Description

This factory-building action roguelike challenges you to master the art of spell-crafting and survive relentless waves of monsters. Create alchemical generators to power your automated spells and unleash destruction on the battlefield!

Features

  • 40+ devastating spells and powerup recipes!
  • 20+ rare totems to enable tons of different fun playstyles and builds!
  • Dash, dodge, and destroy massive waves of enemies on the battlefield!
  • 20:00 demo battle timer - Can you survive to the end?!
